3-29日论文审稿意见

回稿原信

Reviewer: 1

This paper proposes an improved adaptive learning control method for dynamic systems affected by unknown but bounded disturbances. The method focuses on enhancing control performance while ensuring stability. Utilizing the equivalent-input-disturbance (EID) concept, an error dynamic was derived for system analysis, and a Performance-Oriented Neural Network (PONN) was developed to serve as an EID estimator. However, several issues must be addressed before the paper can be considered for publication.

  1. The literature review is not sufficiently detailed. The author should analyze the characteristics and shortcomings of existing active disturbance suppression methods to motivate this paper and demonstrate its innovation through comparison.
  2. The role of equation (9) needs clarification. What distinguishes the dynamic equation (6) from equation (9)? The authors should provide a clearer explanation of the derivation process.
  3. The motivation of the paper should be made more explicit.
  4. Figure 2 does not correspond to Fig. 1, as it lacks the dynamics of the original system (1). This omission leads to incorrect derivations of the stability condition and the design of controller parameters.
  5. The structure of the PONN proposed in this paper is significantly more complex than that of conventional EID estimators, which can enhance EID estimation accuracy by selecting appropriate low-pass filters. Professor She’s research group has developed several improved EID estimator design methods for this issue. Neural networks (NNs) inherently have limitations regarding response speed and accuracy, raising doubts about the justification for simply replacing traditional EID estimators with a PONN.
  6. Many controller parameters must be appropriately selected. The authors should clarify how these parameters should be chosen.
  7. The simulation and experimental sections should provide the main parameters of GEID and the dynamic disturbance compensation gain of GESO to ensure a fair comparison.
  8. The practicability of the method proposed in this paper needs to be strengthened.

Reviewer: 2

It is interesting to have performance-oriented neural network estimators for EID design. The contributions of this paper should be further clarified and elaborated. Here are detailed comments:

  1. The first contribution of this paper is that less information is utilized for the estimator design compared to [14]. However, the information used in [14] is from controller states, they are fully accessible. If more information can be used for the estimator design, why not utilize it?
  2. The authors claim the measurement noise is not considered in [13]. Noise-attenuation is a regular design in a controller design. Usually, a special design should be considered to handle measurement noise.
  3. Assumption 2 is very strong. If g(x) includes unmodeled dynamics and nonlinearity, it is a function of state x(t), and it cannot be treated as a bounded signal.
  4. With assumption 2 considered for the stability analysis, the BIBO stable is not a big challenge.
  5. The comparison seems not very fair, how about the large parameters for L_d and K_d affect other controllers?
  6. More cases, such as reference change, and large disturbance are preferred for the experimental studies.

Reviewer: 3

This paper presents a neural network-based approach for estimating unknown but bounded disturbances in dynamic systems while ensuring stability. The authors build upon the concept of equivalent-input-disturbance (EID) and derive an error dynamic model for system analysis. A performance-oriented neural network (PONN) is proposed to act as an EID estimator, which relies only on system output measurements. To enhance the learning process, an improved weight-update law is introduced, where the main adaptation mechanism is based on backpropagation, and a tunable function is incorporated to balance disturbance rejection and noise attenuation. The effectiveness of the proposed method is evaluated through both simulations and experimental studies, demonstrating improved disturbance rejection and noise suppression compared to existing methods.

However, the contribution of this paper is not clearly stated. The use of neural networks for disturbance estimation has been widely studied, and the authors do not sufficiently differentiate their approach from previous works. A more detailed comparison with existing methods would help clarify the novelty of this research.

Additionally, the system under study is assumed to be linear with a sinusoidal disturbance, which simplifies the control problem significantly. Many traditional control techniques can handle such cases effectively, raising questions about the necessity of a neural network-based approach for this specific scenario. The paper does not discuss whether the proposed method can be extended to nonlinear or more complex disturbances, which limits its practical applicability.

Furthermore, the experimental validation lacks sufficient details. While the authors present figures illustrating the final results, they do not provide enough information about the experimental setup, parameter selection, or evaluation criteria. Without a thorough discussion of these aspects, it is difficult to assess the robustness and real-world feasibility of the proposed method. A more comprehensive analysis, including comparisons with other approaches, would strengthen the credibility of the findings.

分析与总结

  • 文献综述不够详细，作者应分析现有主动干扰抑制方法的特点和不足，以激发本文的灵感，并通过比较展示其创新性，论文的动机应该更明确。
  • 方程 （9） 的作用需要澄清。动态方程 （6） 和方程 （9） 有什么区别？作者应该对推导过程提供更清晰的解释。
  • 图 2 与图 1 不对应，因为它缺乏原始系统 （1） 的动态。这种遗漏会导致稳定性条件和控制器参数设计的错误推导。
  • 本文提出的 PONN 结构明显比传统的 EID 估计器复杂得多，可以通过选择合适的低通滤波器来提高 EID 估计的准确性。佘教授的研究小组针对此问题开发了几种改进的 EID 估计器设计方法。神经网络 （NN） 在响应速度和准确性方面存在固有的局限性，这引发了人们对简单地用 PONN 替换传统 EID 估计器的合理性表示怀疑。
  • 许多控制器参数必须适当选择。作者应该澄清应该如何选择这些参数。
  • 仿真和实验部分应提供 GEID 的主要参数和 GESO 的动态扰动补偿增益，以确保公平的比较。
  • 本文的第一个贡献是，与 [14] 相比，用于估计器设计的信息更少。但是，**[14] 中使用的信息来自控制器状态，它们是完全可访问的**。如果更多信息可用于 estimator 设计，为什么不利用它呢？
  • 作者声称 [13] 中没有考虑测量噪声。 噪声衰减是控制器设计中的常规设计。通常，应考虑使用特殊设计来处理测量噪声。
  • 假设 2 非常强。如果$ g(x)$ 包含未建模的动力学和非线性，则它是状态$x(t)$ 的函数，不能将其视为有界信号。
  • 考虑到稳定性分析的假设 2，BIBO 稳定性不是一个很大的挑战。
  • 比较似乎不是很公平，$L_d $和 $K_d $的大参数会影响其他控制器怎么样？
  • 实验研究首选更多情况，例如参考变化和大干扰。
  • 本文的贡献并未明确说明。使用神经网络进行干扰估计已被广泛研究，作者没有充分区分他们的方法与以前的工作。与现有方法进行更详细的比较将有助于阐明这项研究的新颖性。
  • 此外，假设所研究的系统与正弦干扰呈线性关系，这大大简化了控制问题。许多传统的控制技术可以有效地处理此类情况，这引发了对基于神经网络的方法对于这种特定场景的必要性的质疑。本文没有讨论所提出的方法是否可以扩展到非线性或更复杂的扰动，这限制了其实际适用性。
  • 此外，实验验证缺乏足够的细节。虽然作者提供了说明最终结果的图表，但他们没有提供有关实验设置、参数选择或评估标准的足够信息。如果不对这些方面进行彻底讨论，就很难评估所提出的方法的稳健性和实际可行性。更全面的分析，包括与其他方法的比较，将增强研究结果的可信度。
